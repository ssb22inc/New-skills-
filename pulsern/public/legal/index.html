Educational-use disclaimer

PulseRN is a study tool for nursing students preparing for the NCLEX-RN® examination. Everything in this app — questions, rationales, flashcards, lab reference ranges, AI tutor explanations, and study plans — is educational exam-preparation content only. It is not medical advice, and it must never be used to guide the care of a real patient. Always follow your institution's policies, current clinical guidelines, and the direction of licensed providers in practice settings.

Reference ranges and practice standards vary by textbook, laboratory, and institution. Verify anything surprising against your course materials.

About the content

Practice items are written by humans and by AI, then pass a two-stage review: an automated adversarial review by a second AI model, followed by approval from a human registered-nurse reviewer before they can appear in the app. Items marked with ✨ were AI-generated. If you find an error in any item, use the flag button — flagged items are re-reviewed.

AI tutor explanations and weekly study plans are generated on demand and are not individually reviewed; treat them as study aids, not authoritative sources.

Privacy

We collect the minimum needed to run the service:

  • Account data — your email address and authentication credentials, managed by our infrastructure provider Supabase.
  • Study progress — your XP, streak, answer history, flashcard schedule, ability estimates, exam date, and study plan, stored against your account so it syncs across devices.
  • Aggregate item statistics — how often each question is answered correctly, pooled across all users, with no per-user identity attached, used to calibrate question difficulty.

Questions you send to the AI tutor are processed by third-party AI providers via our server; your study history and identity are not shared with them. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising.

To delete your account and all associated study data, contact ssb23inc@gmail.com from the email on the account.
