How content is checked

Every live question, case study, flashcard, and readiness-exam item goes through a two-layer quality system before students see it:

  • Adversarial AI review — each item is generated by one AI model and then attacked by a second, independent model acting as a hostile examiner: it argues for the wrong answers, recomputes every dosage calculation, and checks currency against present practice standards. Items that can't survive that attack are rejected and rebuilt. The whole live bank is re-audited the same way on a rolling basis.
  • RN oversight — the clinical reviewer of record maintains a review station with full authority to reject or correct any item, and student reports go straight to that queue. Review actions are date-stamped.

AI-assisted items are always labeled ✨ in the app with a reminder to verify anything surprising against your course materials.
